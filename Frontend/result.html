Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a medium-large gun dog that retrieves waterfowl. It has a dense, water-repellent coat and a friendly, intelligent temperament.

More Information

Golden Retrievers are known for their gentle nature and are excellent family pets. They require regular exercise and grooming to maintain their health and well-being. They are also highly trainable and excel in various dog sports and activities.
